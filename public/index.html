Padee

Your bare-bones sketchpad

Padee is a bare bones, no-frills sketch pad for iOS. She's a digital version of that napkin to capture your "Eureka!"" moment, or the margins of your notebook where your characters first begin to take shape. Padee helps you capture those fleeting ideas before they're lost.

Fast

Padee gets out of your way to let you start sketching your ideas quickly.

System Requirements

iPhone: iOS 10
